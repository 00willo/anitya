Monitor the release of your favorite projects

We all have our favorite projects and we all like to use their latest versions with their latest features. However, it is hard to keep in touch with all of them and be informed when they release of new version. Cnucnu can help!

One place

...all linux distributions and even Windows users wants to watch new release of their favorite tool/application.

Cnucnu Web

Monitor upstream releases and broadcast them on fedmsg, the FEDerated MeSsaGe bus.

Use fedmsg notifications (FMN), to set up your own, personalized, notifications of these releases.
